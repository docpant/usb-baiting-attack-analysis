# Contents Analysis

## Information Stored on the USB Drive

The USB drive contained a mixture of personal and work-related files belonging to Jorge Bailey, a human resource manager at Rhetorical Hospital.

The device included family photos, pet photos, employee scheduling documents, hiring materials, budgeting spreadsheets, and work-related administrative files. Some of these files may contain personally identifiable information (PII) and sensitive organizational data.

Storing personal and business files together increases security risks because attackers may use personal information to target employees or exploit organizational systems through social engineering attacks.
